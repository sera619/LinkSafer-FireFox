LinkSafer - Help

Greetings, in the followed small help will explain you how 'LinkSafer' works.

To save a link in the list just open the website you want to safe and hit the 'Link'-Button

To save a notice, click the LinkSafer icon on menubar and enter a notice in the textfield.
After that hit the 'Notice'-Button to save your notice in the list.

If you want delete all your notes/links just click the 'Delete'-Button.

Do you need this list on other place? No problem! Click the 'Download'-Button and get your list as text-file.

If you need help or wish to contact me click one of the buttons.
